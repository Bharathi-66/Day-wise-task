Welcome to Kumbakonam Degree Coffee

The History Of Kumbakonam filter Coffee Kumbakonam degree coffee is a well-known form of South Indian filter coffee that originated in the town of Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu, India. It gained popularity in the early 20th century when coffee
was introduced to the region by British planters during the colonial era.
The unique brewing method of Kumbakonam filter coffee involves using a traditional South Indian
coffee filter called a “dabara set.” The coffee decoction is prepared by allowing hot water to drip
through the grounds, resulting in a rich and flavorful cup of coffee.
